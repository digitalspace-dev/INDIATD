For Fitness event.

  1. Whats the qualifyting criteria for india throwdown Fitness event ?

    2. For athletes registering for fitness event, it will be a two step process

    • India throwdown league
    • India throwdown final
  2. Is the registration process same as other event ?

    3. Yes the registration process is same as other sports

  3. How do I submit score for league event ?

    4. Video recording of all the league event workouts has to be uploaded on youtube and link has to be shared while submitting the score.

  4. How does score validation happens ?

    5. Score validation of league event will be done after the last date of any given workout. Athletes are advised to stick to the deadline to submit the video. After the last date no entries will be taken.

  5. How do I know if I qualified for india throwdown final ?

    6. All the athletes who qualified for the final will be notified by email and leaderboard will be updated with the athletes who qualified. All qualified athletes will have to confirm their presence by paying a amount of 2000 INR.
