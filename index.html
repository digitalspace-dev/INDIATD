India Throwdown

India throwdown is a multi sports event to celebrate the spirit of sports in a festival like environment.
It promotes the sports as well as their athletes. Non athletes are also welcome to try all the sports at india throwdown in their scaled down version.
Our vision is to put not only a world class event in India but to strengthen the bond among the sports community here. Every year we will try to add a new sports and new place where india throwdown will celebrate the spirit of sports with shining athlete.
